MEET THE FOUNDER

A Personal Journey to Peace

My relationship with agarwood began in my grandmother's home in Hainan, where the scent of burning incense marked every important moment—from morning meditation to evening reflection.

Years later, living in the bustle of modern life, I found myself craving that sense of peace. But I discovered that finding authentic, ethically sourced agarwood was nearly impossible.

Serenity Wood was born from a simple mission: to share this ancient treasure with the world while protecting the forests and communities that make it possible.

— Founder Name, Founder

OUR COMMITMENT

Sustainability at Every Step

Protecting Wild Forests

We work exclusively with plantation-grown agarwood. By supporting sustainable cultivation, we help reduce pressure on wild populations while meeting the growing demand for this precious material.

Supporting Local Communities

Our partner farms provide fair wages, safe working conditions, and educational opportunities for their workers and families. We believe that true sustainability includes social responsibility.

Carbon Neutral Shipping

We offset the carbon footprint of every shipment through verified reforestation projects. From our door to yours, your purchase is climate-neutral.
