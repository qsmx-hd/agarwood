OUR HERITAGE

The Story of Agarwood

For over two millennia, agarwood has been treasured across Asia for its profound spiritual and medicinal properties. From ancient temples to royal courts, this "wood of the gods" has woven itself into the fabric of Eastern culture.

From Forest to Fragrance

The Wounded Tree

Agarwood forms when Aquilaria trees are injured or infected. As a defense mechanism, the tree produces a dark, fragrant resin. This natural process can take years, even decades, to produce the precious wood we treasure.

Aging

Years of Transformation

Quality agarwood requires years—sometimes decades—of natural aging. The longer the resin develops, the more valuable the wood becomes. This patience is what makes every piece truly precious.

Ethical Harvesting

We work exclusively with sustainable plantations that protect wild populations while meeting growing global demand. Our partners use traditional methods that have been refined over generations.
